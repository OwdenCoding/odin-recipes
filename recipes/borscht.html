Borscht

Decription

An ideal soup to soothe your winter days.

Let's find out how to prepare Borscht!

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium beets, peeled and grated
  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sour cream, for serving
  • Fresh dill, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Mix beef, onion, garlic, salt and pepper.
  2. Saute onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add beets, carrots, broth, cabbage, tomatoes, and potatoes.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with dollop of sour cream and garnish with fresh dill. Enjoy your borscht!
